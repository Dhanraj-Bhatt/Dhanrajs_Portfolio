SENSOR BASE INTERACTION

AR Interaction

AR Interaction is a sensor-based interactive experience created for the GSEM (Gujarat State Electronics Mission) event, which was focused on the semiconductor industry. In this setup, users stand at a particular interaction point and control the application using their hand movements.

When the interaction is detected, a silicon chip appears on the user’s hand as an augmented reality visual, creating an engaging way to present semiconductor-themed content during the event.
