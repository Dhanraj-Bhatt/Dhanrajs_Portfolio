Exploded View

The Exploded View project is a 3D model viewer designed to showcase the inner components of an Amaron battery. Users can toggle the exploded view to reveal internal parts, interact with individual components by clicking to highlight them, and freely control the camera to rotate, zoom in, or zoom out for different perspectives. This interactive tool offers a detailed and engaging way to explore complex product structures.
This project is currently under development and was created for Amaron.
