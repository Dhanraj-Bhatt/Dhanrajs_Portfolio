IOT Video Player

The IOT Video Player project features a custom video player seamlessly integrated with a circular knob device for intuitive control. Users can rotate the knob to navigate through the playlist, sliding it left or right, and select videos effortlessly. Pressing the knob allows users to play or pause the selected video, offering a tactile and engaging user experience.
This project demonstrates an innovative approach to video playback control using IoT-enabled hardware.
