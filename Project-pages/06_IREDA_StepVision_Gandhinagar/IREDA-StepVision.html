Step Vision

Step Vision is an interactive video and animation player powered by IoT technology. It uses sensors to detect a person's footsteps and plays videos or animations based on their position. There are 4 to 5 standing points, each acting as a trigger to play specific content depending on where the person is standing. This creates a unique and engaging experience for the users.

Benefits of the Project

  • Offers an interactive and immersive way to display videos or animations.
  • Engages users with smart and responsive technology.
  • Combines IoT and multimedia for a unique user experience.
