Products Showcase for HK BIO Science

This project is a PC touch application designed for HK BIO Science, allowing users to interact with the app and explore the company's products and machines. It features a 3D environment where users can move around and view all the products installed on a virtual lab setup. Additionally, a VR version of the 3D environment was created, enabling users to experience the products in an immersive virtual space.

Benefits of the Project

  • Engages users with an interactive and visually appealing experience.
  • Makes it easy to explore and learn about products and machines.
  • Immersive VR experience provides a realistic view of the setup.
  • Perfect for presentations and exhibitions to impress clients.
  • Combines technology and creativity for a memorable user experience.
