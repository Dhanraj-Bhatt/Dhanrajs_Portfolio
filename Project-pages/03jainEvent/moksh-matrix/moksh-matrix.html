Moksh Matrix

Moksh Matrix is a unique twist on the classic Sudoku game. Instead of numbers, players solve the puzzle using 9 different images. The goal is to place each image in the grid so that no image repeats in any row, column, or smaller square. It's a fresh and creative way to enjoy the challenge of Sudoku with a fun visual twist!

Benefits of the Project

  • A creative alternative to traditional Sudoku.
  • Great for relaxing while keeping your brain active.
  • Suitable for players of all ages.

This Project was created for Gayan Kunj Surat Event.
