VR DOJO Demo For Schaeffler

The VR DOJO Demo for Schaeffler is a virtual reality (VR) application developed for the Oculus Quest 2. It provides a virtual training space called a Dojo, where new employees can learn about Ball Bearing testing and the machines used in the process.

In this VR Dojo, users can explore the steps of Ball Bearing testing, practice handling testing machines, and gain hands-on experience in a safe, controlled environment.

Benefit of the Project:

  • Interactive Learning: Employees can learn through experience rather than just reading manuals or watching videos.
  • Safe Environment: New trainees can practice without the risk of damaging equipment or making costly mistakes.
  • Cost-Effective: Reduces the need for physical training setups and allows for scalable training across multiple employees.
  • Faster Onboarding: New employees can get up to speed more quickly with immersive, engaging training.
  • Realistic Simulations: Provides a more realistic training experience compared to traditional methods, helping users better understand the testing process.
