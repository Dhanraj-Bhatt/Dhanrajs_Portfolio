2nd Asia Pacific Ministerial Conference on Civil Aviation

At 2nd Asia Pacific Ministerial Conference on Civil Aviation, I contributed by creating a 3D interactive book titled Vikas Bhi Virasat Bhi, which showcased the development of all Indian airports in a visually engaging format. Additionally, I developed a small TCP communication application for the Drone Federation of India (DFI) to highlight the advancements, benefits, and R&D of DFI drones. I also successfully handled three different projects on the spot during the event(including these two).
