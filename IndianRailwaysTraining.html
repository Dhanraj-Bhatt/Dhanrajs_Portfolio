Indian Railways Training

Indian Railways Training is a VR application created to train railway employees on how to park trains properly so that they remain safely secured and do not get derailed or start moving on their own because of pressure from other bogies or external force.

The training is divided into three stages, and in each stage the trainee must complete several tasks in the correct order. The application tracks performance step by step, and in the final performance summary the user is shown their result. If all tasks are completed in the proper order in every stage, the trainee passes otherwise, the trainee fails.

Benefits of the Project:

  • Safety-Focused Training: Helps railway employees learn the correct train parking procedure in a controlled virtual environment.
  • Step-by-Step Validation: Ensures that the trainee performs each task in the required order across all three stages.
  • Performance Tracking: Provides a clear result summary showing whether the trainee passed or failed based on task completion.
  • Risk-Free Practice: Allows employees to practice operational procedures without real-world railway risk.
  • Process Discipline: Reinforces the importance of following the correct sequence to avoid unsafe train movement.
