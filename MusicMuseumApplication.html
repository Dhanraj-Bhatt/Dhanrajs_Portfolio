PROJECTION & TABLETS VIA UDP

Music Museum Application

Music Museum Application is a musical and visual interactive experience built in Unity using UDP communication. The setup includes one projector on a round table and six tablets placed around it, where each tablet represents a different musical instrument sound.

Each user can play one sound at a time, and when a user pushes their sound globally by swiping up on the upward drag arrow, the other five clients can hear that sound in sync with their own selected sounds. At the same time, beautiful animations are played on the projected table surface, turning the interaction into a shared musical and visual experience.
